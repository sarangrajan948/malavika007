About Malavika Mohanan

Malavika Mohanan is an Indian actress and model who works predominantly in Malayalam and Tamil language films. The daughter of cinematographer K. U. Mohanan, Malavika made her acting debut with the romantic drama Pattam Pole opposite Dulquer Salmaan

Malavika is one of the most valuable south indian actress.Malavika is known as "Dazzling Queen Of South India". Malavika is so popular across kerala & Tamil Nadu with her recent works.
